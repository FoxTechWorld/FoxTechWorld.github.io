Rant: You'll never be productive

The author argues that productivity videos promoting complexity as the key to efficiency are misleading viewers. Instead, simplicity and practicality are the real drivers of productivity. He criticizes the use of complex tools and methods, highlighting that true productivity begins with simple principles: writing, saving content, and reading. By embracing straightforward strategies, individuals can achieve real and sustainable productivity gains.
